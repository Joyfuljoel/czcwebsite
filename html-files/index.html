About Grace Conference

Our Mission

To equip believers for works of service through biblical teaching, spiritual formation, and community building, so that the body of Christ may be built up until we all reach unity in the faith and in the knowledge of the Son of God.

Our Vision

We envision a thriving community of believers who are deeply rooted in Scripture, actively engaged in worship, and passionately committed to sharing the love of Christ in their neighborhoods and around the world.

Core Values

  • Biblical Authority
  • Authentic Community
  • Spiritual Growth
  • Compassionate Outreach
  • Excellence in Ministry

Our History

Founded in 1985 by a small group of believers meeting in a home, Grace Conference has grown to become a regional ministry impacting thousands of lives each year through our conferences, resources, and local church partnerships.

What began as a simple Bible study has blossomed into a movement dedicated to equipping believers for effective ministry in their communities.
